Overview Complexity Types Design Techniques

Core Strategies

  • Brute force: One by one attempt all possiblilities. A slow baseline to compare other algorythims against.
  • Divide & Conquer: Split a larger problem into subproblems, solve individually and then combine.
  • Greedy: Make choices that are most optimal in the moment without concern for the greater problem.
  • Recursion: Define a problem in as an individual base case, and repeate the solution as the case grows.
Recursion vs Divide & Conquer

Recusrion is a method in which a function continues to call itself until a base case is completed. The Divide & Conquer technique can use recursion as a tool for success.
